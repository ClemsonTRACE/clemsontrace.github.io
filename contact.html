Undergraduate Students

Take part in our current research and explore questions you may have in regards to your future in acdemia. Undergraduates are expected to help build and conduct experiments with the goal of later contributing to publications

Requirements

TRACE Lab is driven by students who excel their field. As such, please know we expect the following standards to be met by students interested in joining.

  • 3.0 GPA minimum
  • Faculty Recommendations
  • TRACE Research Group member endorsemen
  • Ability to assist graduate students and Phd candidates in their research without interfering with your studies

Application

To see if you are right for the lab, please send us email us the following materials:

  • Resume
  • Personal Website
  • Linkedin

Graduate students are involved in the vetting of applications. Please submit your application to the graduate student in the relevant field. All applications are sent to Dr. McNeese as well.
